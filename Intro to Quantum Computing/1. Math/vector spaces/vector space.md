>[!info] set with vectors that can be added together and scaled

## Axioms

- $u + (v + w) = (u + v) + w$
- $u + v = v + u$
- $\exists \pmb{0} \in V. v + \pmb{0} = v \forall v \in V$
- $\forall v \in V \exists -v \in V. v + (-v) = 0$
- $a(bv) = (ab)v$
- $1v = v$
- $a(u+v) = au + av$
- $(a + b)v = av + bv$
