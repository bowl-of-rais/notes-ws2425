## Properties

- $(A^{T})^{T}$
- $(A+B)^{T} = A^{T} + B^{T}$
- $(cA)^{T}= cA^{T}$
- $(AB)^{T} = B^{T}A^{T}$
- $\det(A^{T}) = \det(A)$
- $a\cdot b = a^{T}b$
- $(A^{T})^{-1} = (A^{-1})^{T}$
- transpose has same eigenvalues as original
