## Cauchy-Schwarz inequality

for all $v, w \in V$: $$\vert \langle {v} \vert {w} \rangle \leq \Vert{v}\Vert \Vert{w}\Vert$$

## Geometric interpretation

$$\cos(\vartheta) = \frac{\langle {v} \vert {w} \rangle }{\Vert{v}\Vert \Vert{w}\Vert }$$
